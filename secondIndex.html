Welcome!

WeCharge is a revolutionary concept that free E-Car owners from the tethers of limited charge stations. WeCharge is a platform that connects homewowners willing to lend out their outlets and driveway space to travelling electric car owners. By opening up rural areas to electric vehicles, we expand the possibilites of travel. As more and more smart cars hit the roads, the more useful this will become.

The concept is simple. For the fast charge station, prospective hosts will need a 220v hookup in their garage. This enables quick charging of under 2 hours.
